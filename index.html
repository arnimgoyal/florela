Our Story

Twisted by hand, loved forever.

Every piece at Florela starts as a simple, fuzzy wire. With careful twists and a sprinkle of imagination, we transform them into vibrant petals, whimsical creatures, and tiny everyday joys.

We believe craft should be tactile. There's something magical about a velvet-soft petal that never wilts — a little reminder that joy can be made by hand.

About Us

Hi, I am florela.

A tiny studio with very twisty fingers and a soft spot for colour.

Florela started with one yellow daisy made for my mom on a quiet Sunday. She kept it on her desk for months, so I made another. And another. Suddenly friends were asking for bouquets, then keychains, then little lamps for their study tables.

Every piece you see here is twisted by hand, one petal at a time, from soft fuzzy pipe cleaners. No factories, no machines — just slow, careful, very colourful work.

The best part? These flowers don't wilt. They sit on shelves, dangle from bags, light up bedrooms and quietly remind you that someone made this just for you.
